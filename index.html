About the Project

Learn about the problem of optimal ship routing and the significance of fuel efficiency and safety.

This project aims to develop an algorithm for optimal ship routing to minimize fuel consumption while ensuring safety under varying weather conditions. It will be customizable for the Indian Ocean region.

Optimization Algorithm

Our algorithm will optimize ship routes based on multiple factors like fuel efficiency, safety, and weather.

Fuel Efficiency

Optimize the route to minimize fuel consumption, considering ship type and sea conditions.

Safety

Ensure the ship's safety by considering weather patterns, waves, and currents.

Weather Adaptability

Adapt the route in real-time as weather conditions change during the voyage.
